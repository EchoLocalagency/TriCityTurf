Our Turf Services

From residential lawns to commercial landscapes, we handle every type of synthetic turf project in North County San Diego.

What We Do

Comprehensive Turf Solutions

Tri-City Synthetic Turf offers a full range of artificial turf services for homes and businesses across North County San Diego. Whether you need a complete lawn replacement, a custom putting green, or a simple turf repair, our experienced team delivers quality results on every project. Browse our services below and contact us for a free estimate.

Artificial Turf Installation

Complete turf solutions for any residential or commercial property.

Putting Greens

Custom backyard putting greens with true ball roll and professional-grade surfaces.

Pet Turf

Durable, antimicrobial turf designed for pets with superior drainage.

Playground Turf

Safe, cushioned surfaces for play areas, schools, and daycare facilities.

Front Lawn Conversions

Replace your water-hungry lawn with year-round green that saves you money.

Backyard/Pool Areas

Mud-free, slip-resistant turf around pools, patios, and outdoor living spaces.

Bocce Ball Courts

Regulation and custom bocce ball courts with consistent, low-maintenance playing surfaces.

Turf Repairs

Fix seams, patch damaged areas, and restore your existing turf to like-new condition.

Turf Removal

Professional removal of old or damaged turf, with proper disposal and site cleanup.

Surf Mats & Remnants

Affordable turf remnants and custom surf mats for smaller projects and budgets.

Commercial Turf

Large-scale turf installations for businesses, HOAs, parks, and public spaces.

Landscape Design

Full landscape design services that integrate turf with hardscape, plants, and lighting.
