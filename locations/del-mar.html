Artificial Turf Installation in Del Mar, CA

Premium turf for premium properties. Flawless green year-round for Del Mar's finest homes.

Del Mar's Trusted Turf Installer

Del Mar is one of San Diego County's most prestigious communities, home to oceanfront estates, the iconic Del Mar Racetrack and Fairgrounds, and some of the highest property values in the region. Homeowners in Del Mar Heights, the Torrey Pines area, and the neighborhoods surrounding Camino Del Mar expect nothing short of excellence when it comes to their properties. That is the standard we bring to every turf installation. Our team has spent over 13 years perfecting the craft of synthetic turf installation, and we understand that in a market like Del Mar, the details matter as much as the finished product.

Del Mar's coastal climate provides mild temperatures and ocean breezes, but it also brings salt air, morning fog, and the ongoing water restrictions that affect all of San Diego County. Maintaining a lush natural lawn here requires heavy irrigation, regular treatments, and a landscaping crew on retainer. Artificial turf offers a smarter alternative. It delivers the same manicured, green appearance without any of the ongoing cost or water consumption. The turf products we install are specifically chosen for their realistic look, soft feel underfoot, and durability in coastal conditions.

We have worked on properties of all sizes in and around Del Mar, from compact front yards near the village to expansive estate lawns in the hills above the coast. Our services include full lawn conversions, custom putting greens for the golf community, pet-friendly turf zones, and integrated landscape design that combines turf with hardscape and native plantings. Every project is handled by our own crew with no subcontractors, ensuring the quality and attention to detail that Del Mar homeowners expect.
