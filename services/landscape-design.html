Landscape Design with Artificial Turf

Complete landscape design and installation integrating turf, hardscape, plants, and lighting. One contractor, one vision.

Complete Transformations

Full Landscape Design in Vista and North County San Diego

Artificial turf looks its best when it is part of a thoughtful overall design. A turf panel dropped into an otherwise untouched yard is fine, but a landscape that integrates turf with hardscape, planting, and lighting creates something far more impactful. At Tri-City Synthetic Turf, we offer full landscape design services that bring your entire outdoor space together into a cohesive, functional, and visually striking environment.

Our design process starts with understanding how you actually use your yard. Do you entertain? Do you have kids or pets? Do you want a low-maintenance retreat or a fully loaded outdoor living space? These answers shape every decision from material selection to layout. We do not push cookie-cutter designs. Every project is custom because every property and every homeowner is different.

Drought-tolerant design is central to everything we do in North County San Diego. Water restrictions are a reality here, and the smartest landscapes are built around that constraint from the start. We combine artificial turf with native and drought-tolerant plants, permeable hardscape materials, and efficient drip irrigation for the planting areas. The result is a landscape that looks lush and green year-round without the water bill that comes with traditional lawns and tropical plantings.

Hardscape integration is where the design really comes together. We work with pavers, natural stone, decomposed granite, concrete, and composite decking to create patios, walkways, seating areas, fire pit surrounds, and outdoor kitchen pads. The key is how these elements connect with the turf areas. Clean edges, proper transitions, and complementary materials make the difference between a landscape that feels designed and one that looks like separate projects bolted together.

Lighting extends your outdoor space into the evening hours. We design landscape lighting plans that highlight architectural features, illuminate walkways for safety, accent planting areas, and create ambiance around seating and entertainment zones. Low-voltage LED systems keep energy costs minimal while providing exactly the right mood after sunset.

Working with a single contractor for the entire project has real advantages. There is one point of contact, one timeline, one crew that understands the full vision. You do not end up with a turf installer who damages the hardscape or a hardscape crew that cuts into the turf. Everything is coordinated from day one because we are building the whole thing.

Homeowners in Vista, Oceanside, Carlsbad, Encinitas, San Marcos, and Escondido are transforming their outdoor spaces with our design services. Call (760) 681-3517 to schedule a design consultation and start the conversation about what your yard could become.

How It Works

Our Design Process

From consultation to completed landscape, here is how we work.

Design Consultation

We visit your property, discuss your vision and budget, take measurements, and assess existing conditions. This is where the design direction takes shape.

3D Rendering

We create a visual design plan so you can see exactly what your finished landscape will look like before any work begins. Revisions are made until you are fully satisfied.

Material Selection

We help you choose turf products, hardscape materials, plants, and lighting fixtures. You see and approve every material before we order.

Full Installation

Our crew executes the entire project from demolition through final planting and lighting. One team, one timeline, one finished product.
