Turf Removal Services

Professional turf removal and eco-friendly disposal. We get the old turf out so you can start fresh.

Out with the Old

Professional Turf Removal in Vista and North County San Diego

Synthetic turf does not last forever. After 10 to 15 years, even the best installations start to show their age. Fibers mat down, infill compacts, colors fade, and drainage performance drops. When your turf has reached the end of its useful life, you need a professional team to remove it properly and prepare the site for whatever comes next.

At Tri-City Synthetic Turf, we handle turf removal from start to finish. That means pulling up the old turf, removing the nails and staples, extracting the infill, and hauling everything away. We do not leave debris behind, and we do not cut corners on cleanup. When we leave your property, the site is clean and ready for the next step, whether that is new turf, hardscape, natural landscaping, or something else entirely.

Proper removal matters more than most people realize. Synthetic turf is fastened to the ground with hundreds of nails and staples. The infill material, typically silica sand or crumb rubber, is distributed throughout the turf fibers and weighs significantly more than the turf itself. Trying to rip turf out without properly handling the infill creates a mess that is difficult to clean up. Our crew extracts infill systematically before removing the turf panels, which keeps the process clean and efficient.

Once the turf is out, we assess the existing base. If you are planning to install new turf, the base underneath may be reusable with some regrading and compaction. If the original base was installed poorly or has deteriorated, we will let you know what needs to be done to get it right for the new installation. We give you an honest assessment so you can make informed decisions about your next move.

We are committed to responsible disposal. Synthetic turf contains polypropylene, polyethylene, and nylon fibers along with rubber or sand infill. We work with disposal and recycling facilities to handle these materials properly rather than simply dumping everything in a landfill. Where recycling options are available, we use them.

We serve homeowners and businesses across Vista, Oceanside, Carlsbad, Encinitas, San Marcos, Escondido, and the surrounding North County San Diego communities. Whether you need a small backyard cleared or a large commercial property stripped, we have the crew and equipment to handle it.

How It Works

Our Removal Process

From inspection to site prep, here is how we handle turf removal.

FAQ

Turf Removal Questions

Turf removal costs depend on the size of the area, accessibility, and whether the base underneath needs rework. Most residential removal projects fall between $1 to $3 per square foot. We provide free on-site estimates so you know the exact cost before any work begins.

We haul away all removed turf material and dispose of it through proper channels. Where possible, we work with recycling facilities that process synthetic turf materials. The infill, backing, and fibers are separated and handled according to current disposal and recycling guidelines.

Most residential turf removal projects are completed in one day. Larger commercial removals may take 2 to 3 days depending on square footage and site conditions. We work efficiently and clean up thoroughly before leaving your property.
