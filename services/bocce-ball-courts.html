Bocce Ball Court Installation

Custom bocce ball courts built with premium synthetic turf by North County's experienced turf professionals.

Outdoor Entertainment

Custom Bocce Ball Court Installation in Vista and North County San Diego

Bocce ball has been one of the most popular backyard games for generations, and for good reason. It is simple to learn, fun for all ages, and turns any gathering into an event. At Tri-City Synthetic Turf, we build custom bocce ball courts that bring this classic game to your own property with a professional-grade playing surface that requires almost no maintenance.

Every court we install starts with a thorough planning session. We visit your property, measure the available space, and discuss your preferences for size, orientation, and border materials. Regulation bocce courts measure 13 feet by 91 feet, but most backyards call for a scaled-down version. We frequently build courts at 10 by 60 feet or 8 by 50 feet, and we can customize dimensions to fit your specific yard layout. The goal is to give you the best playing experience within the space you have.

The playing surface is what sets our courts apart from a DIY setup. We install a short-pile, dense synthetic turf that provides consistent ball roll and accurate speed across the entire court. This is purpose-built turf, not standard lawn turf repurposed for a court. The surface is UV-stabilized to resist fading in the Southern California sun, and it drains quickly so you can play again shortly after rain.

Site preparation is critical for a court that plays well. We excavate the area to the proper depth, install a compacted aggregate base for stability and drainage, and grade the surface to ensure it is perfectly level. Even a slight unevenness will affect ball roll, which is why this step takes the most time and attention. With 13+ years of experience building turf installations across North County San Diego, our team knows how to get the base right.

Borders and finishing details complete the court. We offer several border options including pressure-treated timber, composite lumber, and stone. The border contains the playing surface, defines the court boundaries, and adds a clean finished look. We also install backstop bumpers at each end to keep balls in play.

Homeowners across Vista, Oceanside, Carlsbad, San Marcos, and Escondido are adding bocce courts as a centerpiece for their outdoor living spaces. A well-built court pairs perfectly with an outdoor kitchen, patio seating area, or pool deck. It gives your backyard a purpose beyond just looking nice.

If you are ready to add a bocce ball court to your property, call (760) 681-3517 for a free consultation and estimate. We handle everything from design and permitting to installation and finishing.

Why Bocce Ball

Benefits of a Custom Bocce Ball Court

Custom Sizes

We design courts that fit your available space, from compact 8-by-40-foot setups to full regulation 13-by-91-foot courts.

Low Maintenance

No watering, resurfacing, or constant upkeep. An occasional brush is all it takes to keep your court playing perfectly.

Synthetic turf drains fast and stays playable year-round. No mud, no puddles, no waiting for the surface to dry out.

Entertainment Value

Professional Surface

How It Works

Our Bocce Court Process

From planning to first roll, here is how we build your court.

Court Planning

We visit your property, measure the space, and design a court layout that fits. You choose the size, orientation, and border materials. We handle the rest.

Site Preparation

We excavate the area, install a compacted aggregate base, and grade the surface to a precise level. Proper drainage is built into the base layer.

Turf Installation

Short-pile bocce turf is laid, trimmed to exact dimensions, and secured. The surface is tested for consistent ball roll across the full court length.

Border and Finishing

Borders are installed using your chosen materials. Backstop bumpers, foul lines, and any additional features are added. Your court is ready for play.

FAQ

Bocce Ball Court Questions

A regulation bocce ball court measures 13 feet wide by 91 feet long. However, most residential courts are built at 10 feet by 60 feet or smaller to fit the available yard space. We design custom sizes that work for your property while still providing a great playing experience.

Bocce ball court costs vary based on size, border materials, and site preparation. Most residential courts fall between $5,000 and $15,000 installed. We provide free on-site estimates that account for your specific yard conditions and design preferences.

Maintenance is very simple. Brush the surface occasionally to keep fibers consistent and remove any debris like leaves or dirt. There is no watering, mowing, or resurfacing required. Most homeowners spend less than 10 minutes per month on upkeep.

We use a short-pile, dense synthetic turf specifically suited for bocce ball play. The surface provides consistent ball roll and speed, similar to what you would find at a professional bocce facility. The turf is UV-stabilized and built to handle heavy foot traffic and repeated play.
