Pet-Friendly Artificial Turf

Durable, antimicrobial turf built for dogs and pets across Vista and North County San Diego.

Built for Pets

Pet-Friendly Artificial Turf in Vista and North County San Diego

If you have dogs, you know what a natural lawn looks like after a few months of heavy use. Brown spots from urine, bare patches from running and digging, mud tracked through the house after every rain. It is a cycle that never ends, no matter how much you water, reseed, or fertilize. Pet-friendly artificial turf from Tri-City Synthetic Turf solves every one of those problems permanently.

Our pet turf systems are specifically engineered for homes with dogs. We do not just install standard landscape turf and call it pet-friendly. The products we use feature reinforced fibers that stand up to heavy foot traffic and digging, a perforated backing that allows liquids to drain straight through, and antimicrobial infill that actively inhibits bacterial growth and controls odor at the source.

Drainage is the single most important factor in a successful pet turf installation. Our systems drain at over 30 inches per hour, which means urine and rainwater pass through the surface almost instantly. There is no pooling, no standing water, and no soggy patches. The liquid drains down through the turf, through the infill, and into the compacted gravel base below where it disperses naturally into the soil.

The antimicrobial infill we use is a key part of the system. Standard silica sand infill does nothing to address bacteria or odor. We use specialized infill products that contain antimicrobial agents designed to break down bacteria on contact. This is what keeps your yard smelling clean between rinses. Combined with regular hosing and an occasional enzyme treatment, pet turf stays fresh and hygienic even with multiple dogs using it every day.

Beyond the functional benefits, pet turf simply looks better year-round. No more brown spots, no more mud, no more patchy grass that refuses to grow in shaded areas. Your yard stays green and uniform from January through December. Kids can play on it right alongside the dogs without getting dirty. You can host guests in the backyard without worrying about the state of your lawn.

We install pet turf for homeowners across Vista, Oceanside, Carlsbad, San Marcos, Escondido, Encinitas, and all of North County San Diego. Whether you need a small dog run along the side of your house or a full backyard conversion, we design the installation around your pets and your property. Call (760) 681-3517 for a free estimate and let us show you what pet turf can do for your yard.

How It Works

Our Pet Turf Installation Process

Every pet turf project is designed around your dogs and your property.

Yard Assessment

We evaluate your property, discuss how your pets use the space, and identify drainage needs. You get a detailed estimate with product recommendations tailored to your situation.

Drainage Planning

We design the subbase for maximum drainage capacity, factoring in your yard's grade, soil type, and the number of pets. Proper drainage is the foundation of a clean, odor-free pet yard.

Pet Turf Install

We remove existing lawn, prepare the base, and install pet-grade turf with reinforced backing. Seams are tight, edges are secured, and the surface is built to handle heavy daily use.

Infill Application

Antimicrobial infill is applied and brushed into the fibers. We do a final inspection, test drainage, and walk you through maintenance best practices for pet owners.
