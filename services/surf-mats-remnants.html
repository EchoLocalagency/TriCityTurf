Turf Surf Mats and Remnants

Affordable premium turf remnants for small projects, surf mats, van builds, and more. Same quality, lower price.

Budget-Friendly Turf

Affordable Turf Remnants and Surf Mats in Vista

Not every project calls for a full turf installation. Sometimes you just need a small piece of quality synthetic turf for a specific purpose, and you do not want to pay full price for a custom order. That is where our remnant inventory comes in. At Tri-City Synthetic Turf, we keep a rotating stock of leftover turf pieces from our installation jobs, and we sell them at a significant discount.

Our remnants are not seconds or defective material. They are leftover cuts from brand new turf rolls used on full installation projects. The quality is identical to what we install on a $10,000 backyard job. The only difference is the size. Because these pieces are already cut and do not fit standard roll dimensions, we price them well below retail to move them quickly and keep our warehouse clear.

Surf mats are one of the most popular uses for our remnants here in North County San Diego. Surfers in Oceanside, Carlsbad, and Encinitas use turf mats in the back of their trucks and vans to keep sand and wax off the interior. A 3-by-5 or 4-by-6 piece of soft, durable turf makes the perfect surface for changing out of a wetsuit, rinsing off, or just standing on in the parking lot after a session.

Van builds and camper conversions are another big use case. Turf remnants make excellent flooring for sprinter vans, cargo trailers, and RVs. The material is durable, easy to clean, comfortable underfoot, and adds a unique look to any build. It is also lighter than most traditional flooring options, which matters when you are converting a vehicle.

Homeowners pick up remnants for balconies, small patios, apartment terraces, dog areas, playhouse floors, garage gym mats, and dozens of other uses. If you have a small space that needs turf but a full installation does not make financial sense, a remnant piece is the smart move. You get premium material at a fraction of the installed price.

Our inventory changes frequently since new pieces come in after every installation job we complete. The best way to see what is available is to call ahead or stop by our Vista location. We can also cut pieces to your specific dimensions if we have a remnant that is larger than what you need. There is no charge for cutting.

Call (760) 681-3517 to check current inventory or ask about a specific size you need. We are happy to set pieces aside if you call ahead.
