FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Artificial turf installation typically ranges from $8 to $15 per square foot, depending on the turf type, site preparation needed, and project complexity. We provide free on-site estimates so you get an accurate price for your specific yard.

Artificial turf requires very little maintenance. Occasional rinsing with a hose, brushing the fibers upright every few months, and removing debris is all it takes. Pet owners may want to rinse more frequently. There is no mowing, watering, or fertilizing required.

Yes. We install pet-specific turf systems with antimicrobial infill and superior drainage that handles heavy use. The turf is non-toxic, resists odors, and drains quickly so your pets have a clean, comfortable surface year-round.

High-quality artificial turf installed by professionals typically lasts 15 to 25 years. The lifespan depends on usage, turf grade, and proper installation. Our turf products are backed by manufacturer warranties for added peace of mind.

We stand behind every installation with a workmanship warranty, and our turf products carry manufacturer warranties ranging from 8 to 15 years depending on the product line. We will walk you through warranty details during your free estimate.

Most residential turf installations are completed in 1 to 3 days depending on the size and complexity of the project. Larger commercial projects or custom features like putting greens may take longer. We provide a timeline estimate before starting any work.
