## The Opportunity

Tri-City Turf has a strong real-world business (13+ years, Costco authorized, AAA rated, great reviews) but essentially zero online organic presence. Every competitor listed above has dedicated location pages, service pages, schema markup, and keyword-optimized content.

With our new 35-page build (12 service pages, 14 location pages, schema, FAQ, blog-ready), Tri-City Turf goes from:
- **4 unoptimized pages** to **35 fully optimized pages**
- **0 commercial keywords** to targeting **50+ keyword combinations**
- **0 schema markup** to **LocalBusiness + FAQPage + Service + BreadcrumbList + Organization**
- **No analytics** to **full GA4 + GSC + phone tracking**
- **No location targeting** to **14 city-specific pages**
- **No service targeting** to **12 service-specific pages**

The competitive gap is wide open for North County SD artificial turf keywords. Big Bully Turf is the only competitor with serious location-page SEO, and they are a franchise/corporate brand. Tri-City's hyperlocal, owner-operated, Costco-authorized positioning is a strong differentiator that just needs online visibility to match.
