Current Website Audit

What Google Sees on Your Old Site

Your Wix site has 4 pages. Here's what Google actually reads when it looks at each one, and why it's not showing you to potential customers.

Your homepage tells Google it's called "Home"

When Google reads your website, the first thing it looks for is a title and a description. Think of it like the headline on a business card. Right now, every page on your site just says the page name. Your homepage says "Home." Your contact page says "Contact." There are no keywords like "artificial turf" or "Vista" anywhere in these titles.

That's like having a billboard with no words on it. Google has no idea what you do or where you do it.

Google has only found 3 of your 4 pages

Out of 4 pages, Google has only picked up 3. Your Products page isn't even in Google's system. And of the 3 it found, none of them are optimized to rank for anything useful.

No tracking means you're flying blind

Your current site has no analytics installed. That means there is zero way to know how many people visit your site, what they look at, or whether they call you from it. It's like having a store with no way to count how many people walk in.

Your phone number isn't on most pages

Your phone number only appears on the contact page. The homepage, the page most people see first, has no phone number and no way to request a quote. The main button on your homepage says "Leave Us A Review" instead of "Get a Free Estimate."
